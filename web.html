JOURNEY ON CODING

With its comprehensive curriculum and supportive community, FCC helped me navigate the complexities of coding. In this article, I'll share my experiences, challenges, and triumphs on the path to becoming a web developer.When I first started FCC, I felt overwhelmed by the sheer amount of information. The initial HTML/CSS challenges seemed daunting, and I doubted my ability to grasp the concepts. However, I persisted, fueled by my determination to learn. Understanding HTML/CSS: As I progressed through the challenges, I began to comprehend the basics of web development. The interactive coding environment and detailed explanations helped solidify my understanding.

CHALLENGES OVERCOMED

JavaScript Revelations: FCC's JavaScript section was a game-changer. I finally grasped the concepts of variables, functions, and object-oriented programming.

Project-Based Learning: Building projects, such as the Tribute Page and Personal Portfolio, helped me apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios.This has been my breakthrough thoughout this journey, I overcame certain challenges during this jouneysuch

Breakthrough

Time Management: Balancing FCC with other responsibilities required discipline and time management.

Self-Doubt: Overcoming self-doubt and imposter syndrome was crucial. FCC's supportive community and encouraging feedback helped boost my confidence. Debugging: Learning to debug code effectively was a significant hurdle. FCC's resources and community guidance aided in developing this essential skill.
